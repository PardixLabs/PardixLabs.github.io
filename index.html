What We Do

Modern software depends on hundreds of third-party packages. Many are outdated, unmaintained, or quietly introduce security risks through transitive dependencies.

Pardix Labs builds developer tools that help you see, understand, and control those risks — locally, privately, and without enterprise overhead.

FeralDeps

A standalone Java application that scans your project locally to analyze dependency health, version drift, and supply chain risk.

  • Scans projects on your machine
  • Detects outdated dependencies
  • Analyzes transitive dependencies
  • Highlights security & stability risks
  • No accounts, no uploads

Why Local-First?

Privacy

Your code never leaves your machine. No uploading source code or dependency manifests to third-party services.

Simplicity

No accounts, no subscriptions, no complex setup. Just download and run.

Control

Understand what you're depending on before it becomes a problem. Full transparency, no black boxes.

Who It's For

  • Student developers learning dependency management
  • Open-source maintainers auditing project health
  • Indie developers & small teams who want clarity without enterprise tools
