FeralDeps

A standalone Java application that scans your project locally to map dependencies, flag vulnerabilities, and show you the safest upgrade path — all offline.

Download View on GitHub

Overview

FeralDeps scans projects directly on your machine to map dependency graphs, identify risk hotspots, and explain why a dependency matters. It's designed for clarity, not noise.

Key Capabilities

  • Local project scanning — no uploads, no accounts
  • Detection of outdated packages and version drift
  • Direct and transitive dependency analysis
  • Risk signals for security, stability, and maintenance
  • Human-readable explanations and summaries

Why It Matters

Supply Chain Risk

Modern software relies on deep dependency stacks. FeralDeps shows you where hidden risk accumulates before it becomes a problem.

Local Privacy

Your code never leaves your machine. Scan offline, get results instantly, maintain full control over your data.

Developer Clarity

Understand what you're depending on with clear, actionable summaries — no complex dashboards or enterprise subscriptions required.

Get Started

Download the latest JAR from GitHub and run:

java -jar feraldeps.jar /path/to/your/project

FeralDeps is currently in active development. For updates or early access, reach out via email.
